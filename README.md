# Diversity of terrestrial malacofauna, favourable habitats, and relationships with mollusc size in urban environments of Sergipe, Northeastern Brazil

Pedro D. Lima, José L. J. Santos, Allan C. Gomes, Rachel P. P. Carvalho, André M. Alves, Camenas V. Barata, Quezia M. S. Araújo, Tiago S. Santos, Luciane M. S. Melo, Suzete R. Gomes, Victor F. S. Lima, José R. S. Silva

pedrodantas714@gmail.com

This repository contains the data required to replicate the results of Lima et al.

# Data

Terrestrial_Malacofauna_Data.xlsx: Records of terrestrial mollusc sampling carried out in the city of Aracaju, Sergipe, Brazil. 

# Relevant columns:

Period: climatic period during which sampling was conducted (dry or rainy).

Municipality: municipality where the sampling took place.

Neighbourhood: neighbourhood within the municipality where the sampling plot was located.

Sampling plot: identifier of the sampled plot.

Coordinates (Latitude): geographic latitude of the sampling plot.

Coordinates (Longitude): geographic longitude of the sampling plot.

Habitat type: type of urban habitat sampled (e.g. urban park, back garden, vacant lot).

Species: terrestrial mollusc species recorded at each sampling plot.
